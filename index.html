Research

My area of research is machine learning, cloud computing, distributed systems, and networking. I am equally interested in designing algorithms and systems with strong theoretical foundations. 
  • Quantum communications:
    • Network protocol stack design in quantum Internet
    • Entanglement routing in quantum networks
    • Transmission control protocol design for quantum Internet
  • Distributed machine learning:
    • Communication efficient distributed machine learning algorithms
    • Flow scheduling in multi-job distributed machine learning systems
    • Device placement to optimize the distributed machine learning systems
    • Applications of distributed machine learning to practical transportation systems
  • Scheduling problems in cloud clusters:
    • Minimize average coflow completion time through optimizing the bandwidth assignment, traffic routing and reducer placement
    • Job scheduling in a cloud system with hardware accelerators
  • Traffic optimization in networking systems:
    • Multi-objective optimization in traffic engineering
    • Network planning in software defined networks
    • Jointly optimize the VM placement and traffic routing in reconfigurable datacenters
  • Other researches:
    • Recovery of larger-scale cascading failure in cyber-physical systems
    • Survivability of optical networks
    • Scheduling problems for autonomous vehicles
